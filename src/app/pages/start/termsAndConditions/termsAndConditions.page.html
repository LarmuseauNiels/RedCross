Dear First Aid responder

We would like to start by thanking you in advance for filling out our First Aid survey. By continuously providing us with information on your First Aid interventions, you are contributing towards making this world a safer and better place.

Starting from the next screen, you will be presented with a survey that will ask you to answer questions about the setting, location, type of injury, type of intervention and the arrival of any professional help during the First Aid intervention you are currently reporting on. Furthermore, we shall also ask a limited set of demographical questions which might have an influence on your knowledge of First Aid. These include gender, age, educational level and whether you have any prior knowledge of First Aid.

No risk is involved in contributing to this study. Your participation is free of charge and know that you can quit the survey at any time without any risks to your person. Your answers will be processed anonymously and we will not attempt to monetize them by selling information to third parties.

Your answers shall be used as input for an ongoing scientific study about the effectiveness of First Aid in general and the First Aid training programmes run by the Red Cross specifically. This might in the future lead to a scientific publication of the data in aggregated form. By clicking on Next, you agree to these conditions and are ready to help us improve our First Aid offering.

Thank you yet again for taking the time to contribute and helping to build a better world.
